Millon Clinical Multiaxial Inventory-III (MCMI-III)

Clinical Guidelines: This inventory consists of 175 True/False statements[cite: 7]. It is designed for use with adults 18 years of age and older who are being evaluated and/or treated in mental health settings[cite: 9]. The MCMI-III should not be used with persons who are not seeking mental health assistance[cite: 9].

Note: The scores generated by this framework represent raw scores based on prototypal items (weight = 2)[cite: 6, 8]. Official Base Rate (BR) score transformations and modifier adjustments require proprietary actuarial tables from Pearson Assessments[cite: 6].
