About Healing Mind

Meet the Founder

John Sebastian

Dedicated to bringing scientific psychology to everyday wellness, John established Healing Mind to bridge the gap between clinical psychological practices and accessible mental health care. We provide a stigma-free environment for holistic healing.

Our Vision

To be a global sanctuary for mental wellness and psychological empowerment, where every individual has the tools to achieve inner peace.

Our Mission

To deliver ethical, confidential, and scientifically-backed counseling services to every individual in need, fostering a safe space for self-discovery and long-term well-being.
